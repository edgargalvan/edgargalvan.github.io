Trend-following portfolio on SPY, TLT, and GLD using Faber's 200-day moving average filter. Monthly rebalancing, no optimization. The core finding: what you do with freed capital when an asset exits is a continuous risk dial, not an optimization. Risk-adjusted returns stay roughly constant from 14% max drawdown (cash exit) to 23% max drawdown (full redistribution).

12+ strategy variants tested across 6 time windows. The simplest version won.
